Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with EarthLungs?

We offer various volunteering opportunities from field work to office support. Visit our Volunteer page to see current openings and apply.

Does EarthLungs accept donations?

Yes, donations are crucial to our work. You can make a one-time or recurring donation through our secure online portal.

Can EarthLungs help with a local conservation project?

We partner with local organizations worldwide. Submit a partnership inquiry through our contact form with details about your project.

How can I stay updated on EarthLungs's work?

Subscribe to our newsletter and follow us on social media for the latest updates on our conservation efforts and events.
