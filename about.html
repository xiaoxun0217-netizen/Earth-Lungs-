Our Core Mission

EarthLungs is dedicated to protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably managing forests, combating desertification, halting and reversing land degradation, and stopping biodiversity loss.

Our Approach

Conservation

We work to protect intact ecosystems through establishing protected areas, promoting sustainable land management practices, and advocating for strong environmental policies.

Restoration

Where ecosystems have been degraded, we implement restoration projects that rebuild natural habitats, reintroduce native species, and restore ecological functions.

Community Empowerment

We partner with local communities to develop sustainable livelihoods that depend on healthy ecosystems, ensuring conservation benefits both nature and people.

Policy & Advocacy

We engage with governments and international bodies to promote policies that protect ecosystems and support sustainable development.
